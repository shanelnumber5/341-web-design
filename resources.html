Need a hand?

The most wonderful think about web development is its community. This field is constantly evolving, and its success heavily dependent on working together.

1. Calendly

My job is to help you learn! If you have any questions about the course content or would just like to chat, please feel free to make an appointment with me. Here is the Calendly Link

2. Moodle Forum

Your first stop for aid is the General questions Moodle forum for this course. I will check this daily, and you can get help from your classmates as well!

3. Self-Help

Never _ever_ hesitate to be your own instructor. There are many resources and communities built precisely for self-guided learning. Here are a few:

  • W3Schools
  • Stack Overflow
  • MDN Web Docs

4. E-mail

Please feel free to send me a message ashley.rezvani@mso.umt.edu I will get back to you as soon as I can!
